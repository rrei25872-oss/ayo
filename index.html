🛡 Stay Safe in the Digital World

Online scams are becoming more common and convincing. This website helps students understand scams, how they work, and how to avoid becoming victims.

Explore Scams

❓ What Is a Scam?

A scam is a dishonest scheme designed to trick people into giving away money, personal information, or account access.

⚡ How Scams Work

Scammers pretend to be trusted organizations like banks, delivery services, or government agencies.

👤 Why People Fall for Scams

Scams use fear, urgency, and emotions to pressure victims.

📝 Common Types of Scams

📧 Phishing Scams

Fake emails or messages asking for passwords or OTPs.

🛒 Online Shopping Scams

Fake sellers offer cheap items that never arrive.

🎁 Prize & Giveaway Scams

You "win" a prize but must pay a fee or give information.

🛡 How to Protect Yourself

Awareness and caution are your strongest defenses online.

⚠ Warning Signs

  • Urgent messages
  • Requests for passwords
  • Unknown links
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers

🧠 Smart Habits

Verify sources, protect personal data, and ask for help if unsure.

💬 A Message for Visitors

The internet can be a powerful tool—but only when used safely. Always think before clicking, verify before trusting, and remember that protecting yourself online is everyone’s responsibility.
