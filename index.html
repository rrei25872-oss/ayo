What Is a Scam?

A scam is a trick used to steal money or personal information by pretending to be trustworthy.

How Scams Work

Scammers pretend to be banks, companies, or government agencies.

Why People Fall for Scams

Fear, urgency, and fake rewards are commonly used.

Common Scam Types

Phishing

Fake emails or messages asking for personal info.

Online Shopping Scams

Items that never arrive after payment.

Prize Scams

Fake winnings that require payment first.

How to Protect Yourself

Warning Signs

Urgent messages, unknown links, and requests for passwords.

Safe Habits

Always verify before clicking or sharing info.
