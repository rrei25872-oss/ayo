Explore Scams

What Is a Scam?

A scam is a dishonest act intended to trick people into giving money, information, or access.

How Scams Work

Scammers impersonate banks, online stores, or government agencies.

Why People Fall for Scams

Scams use urgency, fear, and emotion to trick victims.

Common Scam Types

Phishing Scams

Emails or messages asking for passwords, OTPs, or personal info.

Online Shopping Scams

Fake sellers take money but never deliver items.

Prize & Giveaway Scams

“Win a prize” scams that require a payment or personal info.

How to Protect Yourself

Warning Signs

Urgent messages, unknown links, or too-good-to-be-true offers.

Safe Online Habits

Verify sources, protect personal info, and ask for help if unsure.
