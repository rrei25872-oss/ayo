Explore Scams

What Is a Scam?

A scam is a fraudulent act intended to trick people into giving money or personal information.

How Scams Work

Scammers impersonate trusted sources to deceive victims.

Why People Fall for Scams

Pressure, urgency, and fear are common tactics.

Common Scam Types

Phishing Scams

Emails or messages that trick you into revealing info.

Online Shopping Scams

Fake sellers take money but never send items.

Prize & Giveaway Scams

Fake winnings requiring a fee or personal info first.

How to Protect Yourself

Warning Signs

Urgent requests, unknown links, and too-good-to-be-true offers.

Safe Online Habits

Verify sources, protect personal info, and think before clicking.
